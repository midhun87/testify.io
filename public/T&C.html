1. Data Privacy and AI Proctoring

Your privacy and the security of your data are our highest priorities. We have designed TESTIFY with a core principle of "data minimalism" when it comes to live AI proctoring.

  • No Data Storage: During a live test, the AI proctoring system analyzes your video and audio stream in real-time. We **do not** store any video recordings, audio, or images of you during the proctoring session. The AI models process the data live and then discard it.
  • Live Analysis Only: The AI's sole purpose is to detect potential violations (e.g., a phone, a second person, or background noise) based on predefined parameters. This analysis is conducted live on the platform without saving any of the input media.
  • Violation Data: If a violation is detected, the system will only log the **type of violation** (e.g., "phone detected," "person not present," or "excessive noise"). No image, video, or audio evidence is stored or sent to the test administrator. The violation log serves as a flag for review and is not a record of your live session.
  • Local Processing: All AI processing is performed locally within the secure test environment. The AI models are loaded and run within your browser, ensuring your data is not transmitted to our servers for analysis.
