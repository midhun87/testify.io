Installation Help Center

Having trouble? Here are some common questions and solutions.

My antivirus or browser blocked the download.

This is common for new applications. Our software is safe, but it may not be recognized by all antivirus programs yet.

  • On your browser (Chrome/Edge): Click the "Show all" or "Downloads" button, find the file, and select "Keep" or "Keep anyway".
  • On Windows Defender: If a blue "Windows protected your PC" screen appears, don't worry! This is a standard Windows security check. We guarantee our app is safe and does not access any of your personal data.
    1. Click "More info" (it may be a small text link).
    2. A new button, "Run anyway", will appear. Click it to proceed with the installation.
macOS says the app is from an "unidentified developer".

This is a standard macOS security feature. You can easily bypass this for trusted apps:

  1. Open Finder and go to your Downloads folder.
  2. Right-click (or Control-click) on the "Testify Secure Test" app.
  3. Select "Open" from the menu.
  4. A dialog box will appear. Click "Open" again. The app is now saved as an exception and you can launch it normally in the future.
My token says it's invalid.

This can happen for a few reasons. Please try the following:

  • Go back to the download page and re-copy the token. Make sure you are copying the entire token and no extra spaces before or after it.
  • Test tokens usually expire after a certain amount of time. If it has been several hours, your token may have expired.
  • Try closing and reopening the secure test application, then paste the token again.
The app won't launch or crashes.

Please try these standard troubleshooting steps:

  • Restart your computer. This solves many common issues.
  • Re-install the application. Delete the application, re-download it from the download page, and run the installer again.
  • Ensure your operating system is up to date.
