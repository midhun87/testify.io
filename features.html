Powerful Features for Modern Assessments

TESTIFY provides a secure, scalable, and intuitive platform for creating, assigning, and managing online tests with confidence.

Everything You Need for a Seamless Experience

From test creation to automated certification, we've got you covered.

Comprehensive Admin Dashboard

Get a bird's-eye view of all testing activities. Monitor student progress, track test completion rates, and analyze performance data with intuitive charts and metrics.

Dynamic Test Creation

Easily build tests with a variety of question types, including multiple-choice (single/multiple correct answers) and fill-in-the-blanks. Create a rich question bank for reuse.

Smart Test Assignment

Assign tests to entire colleges or specific groups of students. Automated email notifications ensure students are always informed about their upcoming assessments.

Secure Proctored Environment

Tests are conducted in a strict fullscreen mode. Any attempt to switch tabs or exit fullscreen is logged and reported to the admin, ensuring test integrity.

Detailed Test History & Analytics

Admins can review past tests, see pass/fail rates, and control when results are published. Students can view their own performance history at a glance.

Automated Certificate Generation

Reward success effortlessly. Admins can automatically generate and email professional certificates to all candidates who pass a test, complete with their name and test details.
